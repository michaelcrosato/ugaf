## Executive summary

The demo is genuinely good and near-unanimously so (the opening, the prose, and DEDUCE drew universal praise).
The critique through-line is one sentence: **The Hush threatens constantly and never bites.** Its three
signature systems — the information economy, the re-Settling law-decay, and the climactic escape — are
*advertised* in dialogue and codex but *under-delivered* in simulation, so cynical players keep catching the
seams. 40/40 won and 0 died.

## Ranked backlog (post-#6/#7/#8)

**P0/P1 — the "never bites" cluster (the real story):**
1. **Re-Settling / certainty-decay never bites (~26/40).** The marquee idea is theater: a re-Settled law comes
   back word-for-word; crossing with decayed certainty costs nothing. Fix: a re-Settle must sometimes FLIP
   which rumour is true (gold↔iron) and a stale-certainty crossing must carry a real penalty — or stop firing
   the "your certainty is decaying" warnings (silent friction that resolves to nothing is worse than none).
2. **Never lethal (meta: 0/40 died).** Players broke the most-telegraphed rule (iron in the night Greywater)
   for only a cosmetic change. The one genuinely-lethal law (Antenna → the Changed) is skippable. **UNDER-TESTED:
   careful personas never trigger failure — must add reckless personas (see methodology).**
3. **Antenna Field inert (~20/40).** A headline law is fully skippable; `say <name>` isn't discoverable as the
   way to test the notice-vs-Lyle contradiction; the relic does nothing. Route the core through it, or make the
   say-a-name scare cost something real, or cut the foreshadowing.

**P1 — economy remainder (on top of #6, which made it transact):**
4. **Mox sells nothing only-Mox-has** — his paid "safe hour" duplicates Lyle's free info. Give the purchase a
   real, distinct mechanic or stop selling it.
5. **Eun's observation-trade verb is missing** — the cards promise "an observation OR a coin," but only the coin
   path works. Add a `tell/offer <observation> to Eun` path.
6. **One purchase silently spends all coins, unpriced** — and `give coin` (singular) fails (only `give coins`).
   Quote the price, charge one coin per law, alias coin→coins.
7. **Refusal string is overloaded** — "Nothing I can tell you about that" covers both off-topic asks AND topics
   the NPC just offered to sell, so a paywall reads as a parser miss. Split them.

**P2 — content/polish:**
8. Repeated room headers (settlement/Greywater banners pasted across rooms; post-core text stale).
9. The relic is a dead item (wire into Eun's trade or remove).
10. Hollow Dark deduction uncompletable on the walked path (no third clue); the night WAIT loop is dead padding.

- **8 personas, all three tiers** (the clearest cross-model-axis signal in the cohort — NOT capability noise;
  the same empty-night reality hits every tier, opposite symptoms). **This is feedback/0018 #2 re-confirmed at

### 2. The Mox-debt gate route is a BROKEN PROMISE (P1, P0-adjacent for the keystone — a night14 follow-up)
- **3 personas (sonnet), behaviour-confirmed across all 12: `leaned-on-debt=0`.** An objective content gap, not

- **Fix (acceptance):** broaden the debt-act verb to the phrasings players actually type at the gate —

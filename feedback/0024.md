lack teeth (behaviour REFUTES "the laws never bite": greywater-iron bit 21×, the core-clock squeezed 7

disease, ranked by cross-persona breadth:

- **≥9 personas, sonnet + haiku + opus, HIGH.** 12 of the 22 gate-reachers took it. systems-minmaxer (opus):

  *"lean on the debt, walk out. One button. No final test, no choice, no scramble."*
- **§4.3 cross-check (the hollowness is LOCALIZED):** the other routes WORKED — parser-purist's own log shows
  the CLINK and calls drop-your-only-weapon-to-hide *"the one moment the systems clicked into a real
  decision."* `slipped-the-gate=5` players got a real (if light) decision. **Only the debt door is hollow.**
- **Fix (the next swing, on a branch, swarm-validate, merge only if clean — AC):** leaning on the debt must
  cost something AT THE MOMENT OF USE (a Strider's cut, an exposure/timing check, the Cordon nearly clocking
  you) so it is **never strictly easier than `slipped-the-gate`**. The next swarm must record ≥1 debt-route
  tension beat (a check / near-miss / price), not the current zero.
